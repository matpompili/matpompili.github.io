Postdoctoral Researcher. Experimental Quantum Physicist, Research Software Developer

I am an experimental quantum physicist and a research software developer. My research focuses on quantum computer networks. I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Pritzker School of Molecular Engineering, University of Chicago. During my PhD I built the first multinode quantum network, and demonstrated entanglement delivery using a quantum network stack.
